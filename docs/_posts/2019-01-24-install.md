Installing `mcl` to Disk is currently not possible and there is no "Installer"
to speak of.

However running `mcl` is as easy as:

1. Upload the `mcl.iso` ISO to your favorite CLoud Provider or Burn it to USB.
2. Boot the ISO.
3. Wait ~1s

DONE.
